Irish Network New Orleans

Connecting Irish heritage, culture, and community

Our mission is to promote Irish cultural awareness in the Greater New Orleans area and to leverage the talent and energy of the Irish in New Orleans for the greater good of the whole community. Throughout the year, IN-NOLA organizes events and opportunities that bring people together face-to-face.

From cultural festivals to community service, IN-NOLA builds bridges between heritage and local civic life — celebrating the deep history of the Irish Channel, amplifying Irish-American voices, and strengthening the cultural fabric of the city.
